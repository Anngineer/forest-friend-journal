FOREST FRIEND JOURNAL

Send a note about your day to your magical forest friend, telling them what you accomplished or perhaps opening up about about what's been on your mind.

The Forest Friend Journal application generates a link to display your message on a cute background so you can save or share it.

Need any help coming up with what to write about in the journal letter? Strum the bear's guitar with a click and he'll ask you a question here for you to answer.

About

This is a web application for anyone who could use a little kindness or motivation for self care.

The Forest Friend Journal and other productivity apps like To-Do-Dog were designed to create a soft space for building positive habits and help users take little steps towards what they value in life. If you like this page, feel free to check out my portfolio for more!
